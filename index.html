Hogwarts House Hub

These four houses form an integral part of the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, where students are sorted into their respective houses based on their traits and characteristics during the Sorting Ceremony at the beginning of each school year. Throughout the year, the houses compete for points, and the house with the most points is awarded the prestigious House Cup.
Gryffindor is known for its brave and courageous members. The house was founded by Godric Gryffindor and values traits such as chivalry, daring, and nerve. Gryffindor students are often known for their bravery and willingness to stand up for what is right. Notable members of Gryffindor include Harry Potter, Hermione Granger, and Ron Weasley.
Hufflepuff is a house that values hard work, dedication, patience, loyalty, and fair play. It was founded by Helga Hufflepuff. Hufflepuff students are known for their kindness and inclusivity. They value friendship and are often loyal to their friends. Notable members of Hufflepuff include Cedric Diggory and Newt Scamander.
Slytherin is known for its ambitious, cunning, and resourceful members. It was founded by Salazar Slytherin. Slytherin students are often associated with the desire for power and success. They value ambition and can be highly determined in achieving their goals. Notable members of Slytherin include Draco Malfoy and Severus Snape.
Ravenclaw is the house of intelligence, creativity, wit, and wisdom. It was founded by Rowena Ravenclaw. Ravenclaw students are known for their pursuit of knowledge and their love for learning. They value intellect and are often seen as highly intelligent individuals. Notable members of Ravenclaw include Luna Lovegood and Cho Chang.
